The workbook outputs are based on user-provided inputs, scoring assumptions, industry templates, risk mappings, and model logic. The accuracy and usefulness of outputs depend on the quality, completeness, and honesty of the responses.

RR-framework does not guarantee:

- Business continuity during crisis.
- Compliance with any law, regulation, or standard.

- Cybersecurity readiness.
- Financial performance.
- Risk elimination.
- Correct prioritization for every organization.
