# Disclaimer

RR-framework is a decision-support and diagnostic workbook. It does not replace professional judgment, legal advice, audit, cybersecurity assessment, financial analysis, or certified risk management review.

Outputs should be interpreted as structured assessment signals and prioritization guidance, not as deterministic predictions.

Use Microsoft Excel desktop for final recalculation and review before distributing filled assessment results.
