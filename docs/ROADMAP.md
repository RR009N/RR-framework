# Roadmap Logic

RR-framework converts risk treatments and initiatives into roadmap waves.

## Roadmap waves

- 0–90 days
- 3–6 months
- 6–12 months
- 12+ months

## Prioritization

The roadmap is built from:

- Risk reduction potential.
- Business value.
- Time criticality.
- Opportunity enablement.
- Job size / complexity.
- Crisis relevance.
- Industry applicability.
- Capability mapping.
- Maturity band treatment.
