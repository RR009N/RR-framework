# Methodology

RR-framework v3.0 is a structured business resilience and digital maturity assessment model. It combines:

- Company profiling.
- Domain questionnaire scoring.
- Evidence and confidence adjustment.
- Industry target and gap logic.
- Relevance and applicability gates.
- Capability bridge.
- Risk engine.
- Crisis scenario adjustment.
- Fuzzy resilience posture.
- Strategy package selection.
- Initiative prioritization and roadmap waves.

## Canonical model chain

```text
Company Profile

```

## Assessment dimensions

| Dimension | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Process maturity | How formalized, owned, measured, and repeatable processes are. |
| Digitalization | How well systems, data, workflow automation, analytics, and digital technologies are used. |
| Resilience | How ready the organization is to continue, recover, or adapt during disruption. |

## Domain model

The workbook covers 17 domains and 76 subdomains across company geography, strategy, governance, HR, finance, legal/risk, safety, operations, supply chain, manufacturing, sales, marketing, IT/cloud, business systems, data/AI, cyber/privacy, and quality/portfolio management.

## Risk and initiative logic

The model links questions to risk events and treatments, then maps risks to initiatives. Initiative ranking uses a WSJF-style prioritization logic with business value, time criticality, risk reduction, opportunity enablement, job size, relevance, and roadmap wave.
